To Our Customers, Please Read!
Due to the Coronavirus, here are some rules to ensure the safety of our employees:

1. Check out the menu and call: (412) 687-8000

2. You must pay by card. We will accept cash for large order over $30.

If you order in person:
You must order on the phone and then hand us your card between the two front doors for the payment. We do not accept face to face interaction.

If you order on the phone from elsewhere:
You have to provide us your credit card number and the 3 digits on the back.

4. We pack all the utensils and sauces for you and call you when the food is ready.

5. Once you’re here, we will open the door to give you your order.

6. If you are 10 minutes away, please call before you come. Let us know, when you are here so we can open the door.

Thank you for your cooperation!
- Jason Lin


About Us

We have been serving the neighborhood of Oakland in Pittsburgh since 1996. The best traditional Chinese dishes and noodle soups in town. We serve lunch prices all day long. Call us at (412) 687-8000 right now. Quick, fresh, and tasty. We got spicy Szechuan-style food. We also do delivery with Grubhub and Postmates.
